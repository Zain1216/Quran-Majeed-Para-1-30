The Holy Quran is the matchless Kalaam of that Lord. Who is the only Deity the only Creator and the real Lord of the entire universe. He عَزَّوَجَلَّ revealed this Kalaam [Holy Quran] to the Holy Prophet صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَاٰلِهٖ وَسَلَّم . The blessed revelation began in the sacred month of Ramadan. Some blessed ayahs were bestowed upon him directly. The divine revelation was revealed in stages over a period of about 23 years in Arabic language. The most eloquent language, according to circumstances and occurrences.
